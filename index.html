Company's Overview

Hukum Corporation, working since 2016, is a trusted name in high-quality formwork manufacturing and scaffolding rental solutions, powering India’s growing infrastructure.

We offer a complete range of products including Cuplock, MS Tower, Props, Slab and Pier Column Shuttering, and more—designed for strength, safety, and site efficiency.

With both manufacturing and flexible rental options, we support projects of every scale with reliable, engineered solutions.
