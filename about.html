ABOUT US

Hukum Infra, a subsidiary of Hukum Corporation, is a leading provider of shuttering solutions, delivering safe, innovative, and efficient support systems for construction, infrastructure, industrial, and oil & gas projects. Established in 2016, we have built a strong reputation for quality, reliability, and compliance with global safety standards. With over a decade of expertise, we specialize in designing, manufacturing, and supplying advanced shuttering systems that enhance workplace safety and efficiency. Our commitment to innovation, sustainability, and precision engineering makes us the trusted partner for large-scale projects across India and worldwide.

We build the future of construction.
