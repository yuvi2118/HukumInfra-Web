About Plank

Planks are essential scaffolding components used as walkways and working platforms in construction and industrial applications. These planks provide a safe and stable surface for workers to perform tasks at elevated heights. Made from high-strength steel, aluminum, or seasoned wood, scaffolding planks ensure durability, slip resistance, and load-bearing capacity.

Key Features:

  • High-strength steel, aluminum, or wooden construction for durability.
  • Slip-resistant surface for worker safety.
  • Lightweight yet strong for easy handling and support.
  • Available in different sizes and thicknesses for varied applications.
  • Weather and corrosion-resistant finishes for extended lifespan.

Technical Specifications

Specification Details
Material Mild Steel (IS:2062), Aluminum Alloy, or Seasoned Wood
Available Sizes 230mm – 300mm width, 1m – 4m length
Thickness Steel: 1.8mm – 3mm; Wooden: 35mm – 50mm
Load Capacity Standard: 200 kg/m²; Heavy-duty: Up to 350 kg/m²
Surface Treatment Hot-dip Galvanized, Painted, Powder-coated (for steel); Anti-slip Coating (for wood)
Weight 5 – 15 kg/m depending on material and thickness
Application Scaffolding Walkways, Working Platforms, Industrial Ramps
Compliance Meets IS 2750 and OSHA safety standards
