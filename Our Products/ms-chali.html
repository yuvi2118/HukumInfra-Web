About MS Chali

MS Chali (Mild Steel Chali) is a crucial component in scaffolding and construction. It serves as a horizontal support system, providing stability and load distribution across scaffolding structures. Made from high-quality mild steel, it ensures durability, strength, and reliability in construction projects. MS Chali is widely used in infrastructure development, building construction, and industrial scaffolding systems.

Size Variants and Applications

  • 2m MS Chali: Ideal for small-scale construction, repair works, and lightweight scaffolding.
  • 2.5m MS Chali: A standard choice for medium-scale projects, offering a balance between strength and flexibility.
  • 3m MS Chali: Suitable for large-scale infrastructure projects, providing extended coverage and enhanced load-bearing capacity.

Technical Specifications

Specification Details
Material Mild Steel (MS) with high tensile strength
Available Sizes 2m, 2.5m, 3m
Thickness 3mm - 5mm
Load Capacity Up to 2500 kg
Surface Treatment Galvanized, Painted, or Powder Coated
Weight 8 - 15 kg depending on size and thickness
Application Scaffolding Support, Slab Formwork, and Beam Support
Compliance Meets IS 1161:1998 and BS 1139 standards
