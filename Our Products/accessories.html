Accessory Description
Top Cup A vital component in the Cuplock scaffolding system, the top cup securely locks ledger blades in place, ensuring a firm and stable framework. Manufactured from high-strength steel, it resists wear and tear, making it ideal for heavy-duty construction projects.
Bottom Cup Positioned at the base of vertical Cuplock standards, the bottom cup provides a firm seating for the ledgers, ensuring proper load distribution. It plays a crucial role in the stability and alignment of scaffolding structures, reducing movement and increasing safety.
C-Clamp A robust and versatile fastening tool used to securely hold formwork, pipes, and other materials in place. Ideal for welding, woodworking, and construction applications, it allows for easy adjustments while maintaining a firm grip on the components.
Cup Nut A specially designed fastening nut that ensures a secure and firm connection in Cuplock scaffolding systems. Its unique shape allows for easy tightening and loosening, making assembly and disassembly more efficient on construction sites.
Spigot Pin A precision-engineered connecting pin used to join vertical scaffolding tubes or standards. It enhances structural integrity, ensuring proper alignment and stability in multi-level scaffolding arrangements. Made from high-grade steel, it offers excellent durability.
Rapid Clamp A high-tensile strength clamp used in scaffolding and formwork systems to provide a quick and secure fastening solution. The rapid clamp significantly reduces assembly time and enhances safety by offering a tight grip on structures.
Prop Nut A key component in adjustable scaffolding props, allowing fine height adjustments for leveling and supporting formwork and temporary structures. Its threaded design ensures precise positioning and stability under load.
Prop Sleeve A reinforcing sleeve that strengthens scaffolding props, ensuring secure height adjustments and reducing wear over extended use. Made from corrosion-resistant steel, it extends the lifespan of scaffolding props in demanding environments.
Ledger Blade A heavy-duty connector used in Cuplock scaffolding systems to lock ledgers onto vertical standards securely. It provides structural stability and allows for quick and efficient assembly, making it a critical component in modular scaffolding frameworks.
Jack Nut A crucial element in adjustable scaffolding base jacks and U-head jacks, allowing precise leveling of scaffolding structures. Made from high-quality cast iron or steel, it supports heavy loads and ensures uniform height adjustments.
Nails High-quality construction-grade nails used for formwork, framing, and structural reinforcement. Available in various sizes and finishes, they provide strong holding power and durability in demanding construction applications.
Tie Rod with Wing Nut A widely used formwork accessory that holds shuttering panels securely in place. The tie rod system distributes forces evenly, preventing bulging and deformation during concrete pouring, while the wing nut ensures easy tightening and removal.
GI Agricultural Nipples Galvanized iron (GI) pipe fittings designed for agricultural irrigation, plumbing, and water distribution applications. Corrosion-resistant and highly durable, they ensure a long-lasting, leak-free connection between pipes.
Gugu Machine A specialized construction tool used for operations such as cutting, fastening, or drilling in scaffolding and formwork setups. Designed for efficiency, it reduces manual effort and speeds up on-site processes.
